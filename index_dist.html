Proste Historie
'Simple Stories' is a research project about sociological and spatial aspects of allotments’ gardens in Poland from 19th to 21st century. The conclusions of the study were presented in the form of an exhibition under the Polish section of the first Biennale Urbana in Venice in 2016.

Current contributors to Simple Stories project are:

PAUL CETNARSKI
DOMINIKA DYMEK
MIRABELA JURCZENKO
WOJCIECH MAZAN
RAFAŁ ŚLIWA

Notable contributors to the project:
Maciej Frąckowiak, Marta Kowalczyk, Anna Molenda i Roman Rutkowski.
